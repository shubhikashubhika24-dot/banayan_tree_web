🌱 Amazing Banyan Facts

The Banyan tree (Ficus benghalensis) is India's national tree! It can grow so wide that thousands of people can sit under its shade. Its unique aerial roots grow downward and become new trunks — it’s like a magical forest in one tree! 🌟

Fun-tastic Facts!

🌿 Lives over 200 years!

🌱 Aerial roots become new trunks

🐒 Home to monkeys & birds

💧 Covers 1.5 hectares in India

❤️ Symbol of wisdom & shelter

Did you know? The Great Banyan in Kolkata has over 3,600 aerial roots!
